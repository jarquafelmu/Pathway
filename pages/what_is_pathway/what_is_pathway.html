What is Pathway?

Designed to give students the confidence and skills needed to succeed in college, Pathway is a low-cost educational opportunity that combines online courses with local gatherings. The program takes three semesters, or one year, to complete and can lead to professional certificates and degrees.

  • Standard

    For students who are fully proficient in English, the standard version combines BYU-Idaho online academic courses with either institute or BYU-Idaho religion courses. Students take one academic class and one religion class each of the three semesters.

  • L Version

    For students who are not fully proficient in English, the L version includes academic courses that incorporate English-language instruction as a main component of the coursework. Students meet weekly with a native English speaker throughout the program to improve their English skills. All parts of the program are facilitated entirely in English.
