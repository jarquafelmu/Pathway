Degree Options

Online Degree Program

At the end of Pathway, students may advance into BYU-Idaho's Online Degree Program. To be eligible for the Online Degree Program, students must have obtained a minimum "B" average (3.0 GPA) during Pathway and get an ecclesiastical endorsement from their bishop. (Only BYU-Idaho courses count toward the GPA; institute courses do not.)

Students will continue to take courses online at the same low Pathway tuition rate and can work toward the following options:

Campus

Students who complete Pathway and wish to attend BYU-Idaho on campus in Rexburg must apply at besmart.com and will pay the regular campus tuition. If the student completed Pathway with at least a 3.0 GPA, they do not need to submit a high school diploma or standardized test scores (ACT/SAT).

Other Universities

Completion of Pathway will give students the confidence and skills needed to succeed in any college. Students are encouraged to attend their local colleges and universities at the completion of Pathway if their educational and career goals are better met there.
