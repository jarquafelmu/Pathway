  • Pathway students who choose to continue their education on-campus after Pathway will pay regular on-campus tuition.

Financial Aid

Students who are United States citizens and who have a high school diploma or equivalent are eligible to apply for U.S. federal financial aid after completing Pathway if they apply and are admitted into the BYU-Idaho Online Degree Program. University scholarships are not available to Pathway or other online students.
