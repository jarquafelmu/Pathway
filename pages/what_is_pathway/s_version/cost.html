Standard Version

For students who are proficient in English, the standard version combines BYU-Idaho online academic courses with either institute or BYU-Idaho religion courses. Students take one academic class and one religion class each of the three semesters.

Cost

Pathway students who pursue a degree through BYU-Idaho's Online Degree Program after Pathway continue to pay their local Pathway tuition rate.

Pathway tuition may increase annually.

Pathway students who choose to continue their education on-campus after Pathway will pay regular on-campus tuition.

Financial Aid

Financial aid is not available for Pathway students during their first year. However, U.S. students who have a high school diploma or GED may apply for federal financial aid after they complete Pathway and if they are accepted into a degree-seeking program.
