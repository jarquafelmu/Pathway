Standard Version

For students who are proficient in English, the standard version combines BYU-Idaho online academic courses with either institute or BYU-Idaho religion courses. Students take one academic class and one religion class each of the three semesters.

Caclulate Tuition

Tuition is based on location and the number of BYU-Idaho course credits a student takes each semester. A course credit is a measurement used to quantify the load of work for a specific course and enables universities to set a requirement to graduate. Pathway standard version courses are either 2 or 3 credits each.

In the fall and winter semesters, Age 18-30 groups and Age 18+ groups complete 3 BYU-Idaho online credits at the local tuition rate and 2 institute credits at no charge. In spring semester, these students do not take an institute class, but instead complete 5 BYU-Idaho online credits.

Age 31+ groups complete 5 BYU-Idaho online credits in each of the 3 semesters.
